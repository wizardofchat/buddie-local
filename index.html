§ 0 — Abstract

The Existence Proof

This paper describes Buddie.local — a fully operational, private, multi-tier agentic AI operating system built and operated by a single person, running on a home PC in Northern Ireland, with negligible daily running cost.

It integrates live personal finance, health, and energy data. It routes queries across five intelligence tiers — from instant deterministic computation to multi-model ensemble deliberation. It synthesises observations every morning across all three domains. It has never sent sensitive financial data to a cloud server.

"What enterprise teams spend six months and £500,000 attempting to approximate — one person built privately, for the cost of evenings and hardware they already owned."

This is not a research prototype. It is running live, answering questions, building its own capabilities, and getting measurably smarter every session. The architecture is original, independently validated by six major AI systems, and represents a new pattern for personal intelligence infrastructure that no commercial product currently offers.

The core claim: personal AGI does not require cloud scale, venture funding, or a team. It requires the right architecture.

Machine Builds Machine

The core architectural principle. When Buddie can't answer a question, it logs the gap. After 3 repeats, it asks: "Want me to build a capability for this?"

§ 2 — The Observation Engine

Buddie doesn't answer questions. It notices things.

The trust architecture

Buddie is currently in a 30-day ghost run. Every observation is logged but never acted on. After 30 days, the accuracy record becomes the basis for calibrated confidence thresholds. Autonomous actions only unlock when the track record earns them.

01
No model may both generate and authorize the same action. Generator writes to escrow. A separate rule-based verifier stamps it publishable or not.
02
Observations are not recommendations. "What happened?" is automatic. "What should you do?" requires human approval. Actions require the strictest gate.
03
Privacy is architectural, not a setting. Tickers are anonymised before reaching cloud LLMs. Health data never leaves the device. The architecture enforces privacy — it is not a policy promise.
04
Confidence is earned, not claimed. No self-reported model confidence. Composite score: self-consistency × cross-model agreement × historical accuracy × retrieval grounding.

§ 4 — The Opportunity

Why this matters for India — and beyond.

India has over 100 million retail investors. Every one of them shares their most sensitive financial data with cloud platforms they do not own, cannot audit, and pay monthly for. The data leaves their device on every query. The session ends. The system forgets them.

The personal data economy is broken.

Cloud AI platforms monetise your data, forget your history, and charge you for the privilege. Buddie.local inverts this: your data never leaves your device, the system remembers everything, and the running cost is near-zero.

This is not a niche proposition. It is the architecture that personal finance intelligence should have always had — and never did.

The Buddie.local model is broker-agnostic. The same architecture works with Zerodha, Groww, ICICI Direct, any broker with an API. The intelligence layer is separable from the data source. A retail investor in Mumbai, Bangalore, or Chennai could run this on a laptop they already own.

India's retail investor base is young, mobile-first, and deeply cost-conscious. A system that provides institutional-grade portfolio intelligence at near-zero cost — privately, locally, on hardware they own — addresses a market gap that no current product does.
